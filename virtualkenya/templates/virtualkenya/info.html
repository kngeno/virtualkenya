About the project

VirtualKenya WMS is simple project which shows how to enable nice and easy way for users to query wms map objects based on their attributes.
Although all map objects here are related to VirtualKenya wms it can easily be modifed and replaced with some any map service (depending on your needs).
Please have in mind that this is just basic version and that it could easily be extended depending on your needs.

Used Technologies

    • Python (Django 1.6.5)
  • Frontend
    • HTML5
    • Twitter Bootstrap
    • OpenLayers
